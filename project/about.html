About Spring Lake

Spring Lake Elementary was established in 1966 and has served the community with an outstanding education for many years. The school is located in Altamonte Springs, Florida, which is about 15 miles outside of Orlando, Florida. Spring Lake has been a Five Star School for the past 7 years. Our New Principal, Mrs. Shana Rafalski and Assistant Principal, Ms. Omayra Torres are committed to setting a standard of excellence. The current focus for Spring Lake Elementary is the revitalization of classroom instruction utilizing current technology and research to promote positive collaboration, enhance learning and create a respectful climate that will encourage students to reach their learning potential.
